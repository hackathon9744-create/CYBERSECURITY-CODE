Prevention Steps

  • Enable Two-Factor Authentication

    Add an extra layer of security to your accounts.

  • Use Strong Passwords

    Never reuse passwords and consider a password manager.

  • Avoid Suspicious Links

    Do not click on unknown links or attachments.

  • Keep Software Updated

    Install security updates regularly.

Common Scam Patterns

Urgent Payment Requests

Emails asking for immediate payment to trick users.

Fake Tech Support

Scammers claiming system errors and asking for access or payment.

Lottery / Gift Scams

Promises of gifts or prizes in exchange for personal info or fees.
