Cybersecurity Prevention Steps

  • Enable Two-Factor Authentication

    Protect your accounts with an extra layer of security beyond passwords.

  • Use Strong, Unique Passwords

    Never reuse passwords across accounts. Use a password manager if needed.

  • Avoid Suspicious Links

    Do not click unknown links or attachments from emails and messages.

  • Keep Software Updated

    Always install security updates for your OS, apps, and antivirus software.

Common Scam Patterns

Urgent Payment Request

Emails claiming urgent payments to trick users into transferring money.

Fake Tech Support

Callers or popups claiming system errors and asking for remote access or payment.

Lottery / Gift Scams

Fraudsters promising gifts or prizes in exchange for personal information or fees.
