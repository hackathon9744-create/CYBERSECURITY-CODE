About the Project

CyberSec Intelligence is an AI-driven fraud detection system designed to protect users from phishing links, scam messages, impersonation attacks, and evolving cyber threats.

The system uses a hybrid engine that combines:

  • Machine Learning (Random Forest URL classifier)
  • LLM-based semantic fraud detection
  • Rule-based heuristics for domain & text patterns
  • Fusion engine for final risk evaluation

Problem We Solve

Cybercrime continues to grow rapidly — from fake banking pages, refund frauds, KYC scams, to AI-generated phishing messages. Most detection systems fail because scammers constantly evolve their methods.

Our system adapts by combining statistical ML with LLM reasoning to recognize even new, never-seen cyber threats.
